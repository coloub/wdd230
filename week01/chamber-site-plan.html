Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Sucre, Bolivia

Site Name

Sucre Chamber of Commerce

The name was selected because it represents the city of Sucre, which is the constitutional capital of Bolivia. The chamber will serve local businesses and promote economic growth in the region, especially during the bicentennial celebrations in 2025.

Site Purpose

The Sucre Chamber of Commerce website will serve the following purposes:

  • Promote Bicentennial Tourism (2025): Highlight 200th-anniversary events, including colonial museum exhibitions, traditional festivals (e.g., "Pujllay" indigenous dance), and historical tours in Sucre, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Boost Local Economy: Provide resources for businesses to capitalize on increased tourism, such as guides for artisans, hospitality training, and partnerships with cultural landmarks like Casa de la Libertad.
  • Community Engagement: Foster collaboration between local businesses, government, and cultural organizations to ensure a successful bicentennial celebration.

Scenarios

Key questions from target audiences (tourists, entrepreneurs, historians):

  • Events: "Where can I find the schedule for bicentennial parades and artisan fairs in Sucre this month?"
  • Membership: "How do I apply for a business license to sell traditional textiles during bicentennial celebrations?"
  • Economic Data: "What is Sucre’s current GDP growth rate (3.2% in 2023*) and unemployment rate (4.1%*), and how will the bicentennial impact local businesses?"
  • Cultural Heritage: "Which museums (e.g., Museo de la Recoleta) offer extended hours during the bicentennial?"
  • Tourism: "What are the must-visit historical sites in Sucre during the bicentennial?"

*Source: National Institute of Statistics of Bolivia (INE), 2023

Color Schema

The following colors will be used in the site:

  • Primary Color: #0077b6 (Used for headings and accents)
  • Secondary Color: #023e8a (Used for backgrounds and buttons)
  • Text Color: #333 (Used for body text)

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Headings: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
  • Body Text: 'Roboto', sans-serif
