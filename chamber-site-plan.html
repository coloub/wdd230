Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Sucre, Bolivia

Site Name

Sucre Chamber of Commerce

The name was selected because it represents the city of Sucre, which is the constitutional capital of Bolivia. The chamber will serve local businesses and promote economic growth in the region.

Site Purpose

The Sucre Chamber of Commerce website will serve the following purposes:

  • Promote local businesses and attract tourism to Sucre.
  • Provide resources and information for entrepreneurs and established businesses in the region.

Scenarios

Here are some common scenarios for visitors to the site:

  • What events are happening this month to promote local businesses?
  • How can I contact the chamber for membership information?
  • What are the current economic statistics for Sucre?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used in the site:

  • Primary Color: #0077b6 (Used for headings and accents)
  • Secondary Color: #023e8a (Used for backgrounds and buttons)
  • Text Color: #333 (Used for body text)

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Headings: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
  • Body Text: 'Roboto', sans-serif
